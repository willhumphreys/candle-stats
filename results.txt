If today is positive what are the odds the next candle takes out its high 66%  62% 65%

So if a candle closes positive there are 2 to 1 odds that its high will be taken out the next day.
If a candle closes negative there are 2 to 1 odds its low will be taken out the next day.

The odds that the next day closes positive or negative are 50% but the take out is 2 to 1.
